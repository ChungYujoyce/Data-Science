trailing him by four points in ohio the polls have margins of error of plus or minus three percentage points at this juncture trump is doing better in pennsylvania than the g o p nominees in 2008 and party s presumptive nominee last week after senator ted cruz of texas and gov john kasich of ohio dropped out of the race mrs clinton holds a substantial delegate lead over senator bernie sanders of vermont in the democratic contest but he is continuing to compete in coming primaries including tuesday s democratic voting contest in west virginia the polls do offer some support for mr sanders s argument to stay in the race as he outperforms mrs clinton in head to head matchups against mr trump in the three states the senator from vermont tops mr trump by two points in florida and ohio and by
