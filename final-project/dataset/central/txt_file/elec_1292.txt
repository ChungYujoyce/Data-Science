votes for democratic candidates who quit can affect how many delegates the remaining candidates will be allotted party rules dictate that a candidate must earn 15 of the statewide vote to get any statewide delegates and also that a candidate must earn 15 of the vote in any of the state s congressional districts in order to receive any delegates apportioned at the district level that means zombie votes could cause other candidates to miss the 15 threshold mitchell used michael bloomberg in a hypothetical if bloomberg hypothetically received 13 8 of the vote but candidates no longer
